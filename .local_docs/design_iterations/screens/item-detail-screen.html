Notes

Beautiful tall vase with subtle texture work around the rim. The piece was thrown on the wheel with extra attention to wall thickness consistency.

Fired to cone 6 in the electric kiln. The celadon glaze developed beautifully with some interesting color variation where it pooled at the base. Very pleased with how this piece turned out - it has a lovely presence and the proportions feel just right.

Technical Notes: Used about 3 lbs of clay. Trimmed when leather hard. Bisque fired to cone 04. Applied glaze in three coats with careful attention to the rim area.
