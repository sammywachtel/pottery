Layout Strategy

How it handles aspect ratios:

Each photo uses object-fit positioning tailored to pottery photography: portrait photos align to top (catching the rim/opening), landscape photos center-crop (catching the full width), square photos fit perfectly. A subtle "tap" indicator shows users they can view the uncropped version.

✓ Pros

  • Consistent, predictable grid rhythm
  • All cards same height = clean vertical scroll
  • Space-efficient: fits more items on screen
  • Easy to scan quickly (eyes don't jump around)
  • Smart crop shows most important part (top of pottery)

✗ Cons

  • Some cropping occurs (though intentional)
  • Landscape photos lose horizontal context
  • Requires tap interaction to see full photo
  • May hide important details in cropped areas
  • Less visual variety in the grid
