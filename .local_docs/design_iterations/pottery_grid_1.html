Variation 1: Adaptive Column Span

Layout Strategy

Smart width detection: Landscape photos (wider than tall) automatically span both columns for full-width display, while portrait and square photos stay in single columns. Uses CSS Grid with grid-auto-flow: dense to fill gaps intelligently.

How it handles aspect ratios:

Each photo's container uses aspect-ratio-based padding to maintain its natural proportions without cropping. Landscape = 16:9, Portrait = 3:4, Square = 1:1. The grid adapts the card width (1 or 2 columns) based on orientation.

✓ Pros

  • Zero cropping - all photos shown completely
  • Landscape photos get proper prominence
  • Dense packing minimizes whitespace
  • Natural feel when scrolling (variety in layout)
  • Easy to implement in Flutter with SliverGrid

✗ Cons

  • Card heights vary significantly (can feel uneven)
  • Landscape photos break the 2-column rhythm
  • Grid ordering can be unpredictable with 'dense' packing
  • Portrait photos may look small next to landscape ones
